EDEC Feeding Program

  • Home
  • /
  • EDEC Feeding Program

We feed 600 children in 20 ECDE centers to enable them maintain nutritional status. The parents are unable to feed them either because they have no income or because they have to seek casual work and they have no one to leave the children with.

We feel that this is a very important program for the community. It prevents the children from going to the streets to beg and provides them with a very essential basic right of life. Children will develop physical and mentally if only they are well fed. Their parents are unable to feed them, so the programs steps in to fill that gap.
