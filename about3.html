About Us

We Are The Leaders In Consultancy Projects!

Keen Solutions Ltd provides training, counseling, strategy development, project management and other services to corporate, Schools, colleges, NGOs and community programs in Kenya and in the region. Keen Solutions operated as Keen Counseling and Training Consultancy registered as a business name for 7 years. In 2015 it was incorporated as a company under the Company’s Act. Though relatively young, Keen Solutions has had an opportunity to carry out several consultancy projects with great success. Keen Solutions draws its strength and expertise from the diverse skill sets of its Consultants.

Keen Solutions work with a pool of consultants with varied experiences and background. Depending on the assignment we call upon the consultants dedicated to our service. They range from strategy development experts, project evaluation experts, human resource experts, Psychologist, trainers and Business management experts among others. We have provided service to Government agencies, private sector companies, community development organizations, County government departments, schools and colleges. Currently we are running 3 community programs for small scale businesses, a Home based care program for the poor sick and elderly people and a feeding program for 1000 children in the ECDE in Ruiru Sub County in partnership with MFH funded by USAID.

Keen Solutions also run mentorship program in schools in Kiambu County. Our staff have developed curriculum for various organization and have developed guidelines for Ministry of Education and UNESCO among others Keen Solutions developed staffing policy for Mukuru Slums Development Project and reviewed their gender, Safety, Child Protection and HIV/AIDS policies. We also carried out a Job evaluation and developed a salary matrix for the organization that has 56 staff members. In 2012 we developed a drug and alcohol policy for a Government agency; Centre for Mathematic Science and Technology for Education in Africa (CEMSTEA). We trained their Drug and Alcohol prevention committee on prevention, counseling and Management. We are their preferred service provider for training in this area.
